MyCityTown Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

The MyCityTown Chamber of Commerce serves as a platform to support local businesses, foster economic growth, and enhance the overall community development. Our website aims to provide valuable resources, networking opportunities, and promote the city's economic prosperity.

Scenarios

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What has been the population growth in the area?

Color Schema

The color scheme of our website reflects the vibrancy and professionalism of our city. We have chosen the following colors:

  • Primary Color: #FF6347 (Used for headings)
  • Secondary Color: #4169E1 (Used for buttons and accents)

Typography

We have carefully selected the following fonts for our website:

  • Heading Font: Montserrat
  • Body Font: Open Sans
