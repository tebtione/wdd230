Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

Saratoga County Chamber of Commerce

This name was selected to represent Saratoga, emphasizing the role of the chamber in supporting local businesses and community development.

Site Purpose

The Saratoga County Chamber of Commerce is:

  • the oldest sporting venue in the US
  • the home of Historic battlefields
  • a hub for World-class entertainment spots
  • commmitted to maintain a vibrant Saratoga County community since 1918
  • Supported by the Saratoga County Chamber of Commerce

Scenarios

Potential questions from site visitors include:

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What has been the population growth in the area?
